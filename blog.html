My Hobbies

Blue Ridge Mountain Cabins

This area has proudly served as my summer getaway for the past 3 years straight. If you've never been, I highly recommend as it provides a great source of solace from the stress of every day life. Most of the cabins in this area are very decorative and modern looking...oh and the view ain't half bad!

Senoia, GA

This was a very fun place to visit especially if you're into The Walking Dead like I am. A big portion of the show was (and still is!) filmed at this location. There is also a great souvenir shop that contains many exclusive and iconic items from the show!

Jerome Bettis Grille 36

Located right in the heart of Pittsburgh (aka Steel City) and literally next to one of the three rivers lies one of the best restaurants I have ever been to. Although I would advise going during the offseason otherwise you won't even get close to getting eating in a reasonable amount of time. Either way the food is amazing!
